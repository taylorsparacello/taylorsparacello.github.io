atl restaurant crawl

eating my way around ATL using the Atlanta Dining Passbook

Allora August 25, 2018
I stopped here for a weekend brunch with my mom, and I ordered Chicken and Biscuit which came with a buttermilk biscuit, chicken tenders, and sausage gravy. It was just the homemade, belly-filling food I was craving. The environment was welcoming and had a modern, industrial design. The music was trendy and really put the finishing touch on a perfect brunch spot!

Kuroshio Midtown Sushi and Lounge August 24, 2018
Located in the heart of Midtown Atlanta, Kuroshio has some of the best sushi around. I started off my meal with the Japanese Hot Rock with six cuts of seasoned beef tenderloin paired with garlic ponzu sauce. I've never tried the hot rock before, but I won't go here without getting it next time! For my main course, I ordered a simple shrimp tempora sushi with crab stick and spicy mayo on top. Great food, great staff, great vibe. **This restaurant is not in the Atlanta Dining Passbook.

La Fonda Latina July 27, 2018
Bustling hangout with a patio serving amazing salsa, Cuban sandwiches, & other Mexican standards. Atmosphere was great; however the service was less than great. Pictured is the shrimp quesadilla with corn and cheese accompanied with black beans and rice. **This restaurant is not in the Atlanta Dining Passbook.
