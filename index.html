Featured Food

The Shed BBQ & Blues Joint

Ocean Springs, MS

The Shed is... an experience. From the moment you drive up, you see the... interesting architecture and smell the sweet smell of good BBQ. There's random objects, such as old license plates, and Shed philosophies hanging from the ceiling. The Shed is not a fancy restaurant by far; it is truly a joint. "ShedHeads", as their fans are called, bring their families to eat some of the best BBQ in the South. On Friday and Saturday nights, live blues music radiates from the stage and with it comes the true essence of old-school, southern charm. Pictured on right: pulled pork with G-Maw's baked beans and buttered corn on the cob.

bartaco

Atlanta, GA

From the open air feel to the beachy light fixtures, bartaco is a true gem of Atlanta. The California street style food and the coastal vibes provide the perfect place to relax and unwind with friends. Pictured on left: crispy rock shrimp tacos and grilled corn off the cob.
